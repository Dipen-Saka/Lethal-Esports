ABOUT US

Hello! I'm Dipen Saka from Lethal Esports India

Founded in December 2020, Lethal eSports India is one of the fastest growing eSports Organisation in India, having a team of veteran and very experienced gamers and well known streamers on board. Lethal eSports India was founded with the vision of taking the Indian eSports community to the great heights of International Level by empowering the underdog gamers to reveal and showcase their true potential and talent on various public platforms. We see a vision where every Indian Gamer has opportunities to shine brighter
